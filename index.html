Battleship

Hello! Thank you for playing Battleship!

Rules:

In this game you take turns guessing where your enemy's ships are located by clicking squares on the enemy's grid (Computer).

If you successfully hit a ship, the square will be marked with an 'X' and turn red. If you miss, the square will be marked with an 'O'. The computer will then take a turn and try to hit your ships, which are marked with an 'S'.

The game will end when you have sunk all of your enemy's ships, or when they have sunk all of yours! Have fun!

Instructions:

When you click the button below, you will place five ships:

  • The Carrier (5 squares long)
  • The Battleship (4 squares long)
  • The Destroyer (3 squares long)
  • The Submarine (3 squares long)
  • The Patrol Boat (2 squares)

Ships can be placed horizontally (left and right) or vertically (up and down). Ships cannot be placed diagonally, or on top of each other.

After clicking the button you will be prompted ten times to enter in the start and end coordinates of your five ships. Use the grid shown below to place your ships.
